Education and Appointments

I have been an assistant professor at the University of Arkansas since 2022.

Previously, I held postdoctoral positions at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn, Germany and Michigan State University in East Lansing, MI.

I earned my PhD from Brandeis University where my advisor was Danny Ruberman.

As an undergraduate, I attended New College of Florida where I studied mathematics and art.
